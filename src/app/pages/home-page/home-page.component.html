A novel, fully-automated hydroponics system that grows plants up to 31% faster
What is S2?
Our robotic system comprises of a lifting and lowering platform elevator which periodically douses the root system of a set of plants in a aqueous nutrient solution while tracking and adjusting the chemical composition of the liquid. We gather meaningful data regarding the state of the plants via a large onboard sensor suite and the utilization of various computer vision and machine learning algorithms for tasks like 3D mapping, foliage segmentation, disease detection, and foliage prediction.
Furthermore, we have a webapp which allows us to continuously monitor the key parameters of the system. Other aspects of our project include the full 3d design of the mechanical elevator of our system, system electronics, mechanism control theory, and more.
